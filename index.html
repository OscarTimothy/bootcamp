ABOUT THE DESIGNERS

History Of The NACOSS BOOTCAMP

The NACOSS Bootcamp is an Annual Event held in the UNIZIK Chapter where digital skills are being offered to Computer Science students for free. Duration ranges between four weeks to six weeks. This has been a tradition since 2015 and every Regime uses this as a means of giving back to the society. Courses Taught in Bootcamp range from Programming to graphic design and other skills too. The Bootcamp Ends with a little exhibition from the Students

Our Mission

The mission of the bootcamp is to give students a more practical view of what is being taught in class. A way to impact them with digital & tech skills. The Mission is to build a strong Tech Community among students

.

Our Achievements

The NACOSS Bootcamp has served as a platform for so many students to grow and the following achievements have been marked:

  • The Bootcamp has reduced the failure rate in CS Courses by 15% Since it's inception
  • Bootcamp has trained and given birth to Ideas among students
  • Improved Collaboration amongst Students
  • Served as a Bride between different Levels

Our Vision

Bootcamp was introduced for giving out digital skills but it has become more than that. Our vision for the bootcamp include:

  • To promote collaborations between Students and Lecturers
  • To reach out to not just Computer Science students but the school at large
  • To gain recognition from different Organizations for doing what we love

Bootcamp'19

The 2019 Bootcamp kicked off on the 26th July, 2019 It started an opening class by the President of NACOSS, Okolo Oscar-Timothy on Computer Science and the different areas. The Instructors were introduced to their students and were given time to talk about the different packages that were going to be taught. The Bootcamp ran for a period of 4 weeks and ended on the 31st August, 2019 With light refreshments and a class titled; What Next? What you don't learn in School and that marked the end of the Bootcamp
